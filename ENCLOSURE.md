# Enclosure research

## Selected prototype enclosure

**[Luxe Acrylic Football Display Cube — The Container Store](https://www.containerstore.com/s/office/craft-hobby/acrylic-football-premium-display-cube/12d?productId=11004074)**

- Current observed price (2026-06-18): **$31.99 sale / $39.99 regular**
- Exterior: **12.25 × 7.75 × 7.75 in** (311 × 197 × 197 mm)
- Construction: premium clear acrylic
- Access: tight-fitting lid
- Feet: silicone
- SKU: **10068666**
- Reviews: 4.2/5 from 5 reviews at time of research

### Why this one

- The actual product is close to the 300 × 200 × 200 mm concept dimensions.
- It is a finished consumer product, not a custom acrylic fabrication project.
- The removable lid gives access to the complete mechanism.
- The long 12.25-inch axis supports a left-to-right optical path.
- It is locally replaceable and inexpensive enough to drill or notch only the lid.
- A removable internal insert can carry every component; nothing needs permanent attachment to the clear shell.

### Proposed use

Turn the enclosure so the removable lid acts as the bottom. Place a removable 3 mm black acrylic, plywood, or 3D-printed insert on that lid. Mount the optical mechanism and electronics to the insert. Route USB-C through a small notch in the lid or use an internal battery during a short demo.

Before purchase, verify the **interior** dimensions and how the lid engages the cube. The manufacturer currently publishes exterior dimensions only.

## Alternatives considered

### Hubert Wide Acrylic Cube

**[12 × 8 × 8 in five-sided acrylic cube](https://www.hubert.com/product/75921/Wide-Acrylic-Cube-12-x-8-x-8-W-x-D-x-H)**

- Observed price: **$48.56**
- Shipping shown by Google Shopping: **$32.31**
- 12 × 8 × 8 in
- 3/16-inch acrylic
- Five-sided, open bottom
- 1.8 lb

Mechanically ideal, but approximately $80.87 delivered at the observed price. The Container Store cube is the better first prototype.

### Low-cost marketplace flat-pack cases

Search results showed 250 × 200 × 150 mm cases around **$13.50–$25** from Shein, Temu, and similar marketplaces. These are not selected because listings, material thickness, tolerances, availability, and replacement consistency are unreliable.

### Custom museum cases

Custom and museum-style cases from shopPOPdisplays appeared around **$37–$180+**, depending on dimensions and base. They are unnecessary before the optical layout is proven.

## Enclosure constraints for the next mockup

- Model the actual 311 × 197 × 197 mm outer envelope.
- No fabricated pedestal.
- One removable internal insert.
- One moving faceted mirror—not five prisms.
- One optional fixed dichroic tile to amplify color variation.
- One frosted input aperture with no direct room image.
- One frosted projection plane.
- Camera behind a hood, pointed only at the projection plane.
- CoreS3 shown at its real 54 × 54 × 15.5 mm scale.
- All wiring and motor drive below or behind the insert.
