# Contributing

Issues and focused pull requests are welcome.

Before proposing a change:

- Keep the project portable and single-purpose.
- Do not claim cryptographic entropy without evidence and review.
- Do not commit credentials, private telemetry, internal documentation, or camera captures containing people, desks, screens, or badges.
- Prefer real hardware and real APIs over mocks in integration work.
- Document safety, privacy, and failure behavior for hardware changes.
